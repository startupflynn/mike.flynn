Improving Product Experiences

Hello! My name is Mike Flynn, I'm a user experience researcher and designer with an interest in helping early- to mid-stage startup companies advance design concepts from idea to implementation.

Within the wear multiple hats world of UX, I’ve worked on web, mobile, hardware, video production, brochure collateral, tradeshow exhibits, Executive demos, and more. But my foundation—where my core value comes into play—is in applying UX methods to meaningfully improve products and services.

In addition to 9+ years practicing, a Masters in Human Factors in Information Design (i.e., usability) from Bentley University, an in-progress MBA from Babson College, and a technical writing background help add strength and speed to my product foundation.

Making An Impact

When it comes to meaningfully influencing a product or service, the role of a user experience researcher and designer can vary greatly. The realities of each project determine if I'm front-and-center or waiting at the ready.

To me, this means I need to be equipped with usability methods (e.g., what and why) as well as design sensibilities to move beyond mere discovery (e.g., how to design solutions).

Here are a few high-level glimpses into the range of my work--from needs assessments of what life is like within a product space (connected photo frames), to sketching out solutions that 'wow' (online product gallery), to helping find what works (smart home app), and much, much more.
